RULES

Stop the invasion. Save the planet.

Keep the aliens from landing on Earth by shooting their lead spaceships (hint: they're the ones with equations that match the answers on the blaster).

Beat each level by taking down as many ships as you can before the timer runs out. Be careful! If any ship touches the canon or table then you lose!

Mobile users must press the correct ship to shoot. Desktop users must use the left and right arrow keys to navigate, then press the spacebar to shoot.
